## ğŸ’¡ Best Practices

### Sourcing & Screening:
- Add candidates as soon as you make initial contact
- Update status promptly as candidates progress
- Use "On Hold" for candidates waiting for responses
- Schedule interviews directly from this tab

### Recruitment & Hiring:
- Move candidates here only after all interviews are complete
- Make hiring decisions promptly to avoid delays
- Use "Awaiting" status for candidates under consideration
- Update to "Hired" only when you're ready to send offer

### Onboarding:
- Move candidates here immediately after offer acceptance
- Update onboarding stage as each step completes
- Track progress percentage to identify bottlenecks
- Complete all stages before joining date

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: When should I move a candidate from Sourcing to Hiring tab?**
A: When their interview status is "Finalized" - meaning all interview rounds are complete.

**Q: How do candidates appear in Onboarding tab?**
A: They need `hiringStatus: "Hired"` AND `offerStatus: "Accepted"` (or status: "Hired").

**Q: Can I edit candidate information in any tab?**
A: Yes, you can edit candidates in any tab. The data is shared across all tabs.

**Q: What happens if I reject a candidate in Hiring tab?**
A: They remain in the Hiring tab with status "Rejected" for record-keeping, but won't appear in Onboarding.

**Q: How do I track a candidate's progress?**
A: Use the status fields and onboarding stages. The system automatically calculates KPIs and progress percentages.
